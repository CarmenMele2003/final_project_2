Batman: The Animated Series


Aired from 1992 to 1995

Batman: The Animated Series was based on the DC Comics superhero Batman. Warner Bros. Animation produced the series, which initially aired on the Fox Kids Block airing from September 5, 1992, until September 15, 1995. The series received widespread acclaim for its philosophical complexity, gloomy tone, artistic excellence, and fidelity to the character's crime-fighting roots.

The highest rated episode was “Almost got ‘im”, which aired on November 10th, 1992, with a 9.2/10. The lowest rated episode was “I’ve Got Batman In My Basement”, which aired September 30th, 1992, with a 6.5/10.
