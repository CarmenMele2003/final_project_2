About the DCAU


Aired from 1992 to 2006

The DCAU was co-produced by Mr. Bruce Timm and Mr. Paul Dini in association with Warner Brothers. They had help from people like Alan Burnett, Boyd Kirkland, Dwayne McDuffie, and Eric Radomski; each of whom would rotate between Producer, Writer, or Director throughout the run of the DCAU. Andrea Romano and Shirley Walker helmed Voice Direction and Orhcestra Conduction each respectively.

The DCAU spans eight shows and four movies that share the same art design and voice cast. There are however, two recent movies that fans debate whether they are canon; for simplicity's sake, we will not include them in our review. Also, for right now we can only review the 5 top-rated shows or movies.

Our List


Batman: The Animated Series

Batman and Robin fight classic Batman villains and expand the team with Batgirl near the end. 9.0/10


Superman: The Animated Series

Superman fights classic villains from his rogues gallery and has help from his coworkers and cousin. 8.1/10


The New Batman Adventures

With a new and younger Robin and Batgirl, Batman fights crime and teams up with Superman. 8.4/10


Justice League

Batman, Superman, Wonder Woman, John Stewart Green Lantern, Wally West Flash, Hawkgirl, and Martian Manhunter form the Justice League. 8.6/10


Justice League Unlimited

The original seven Justice League members expand its team by over fifty new members and faces the newly formed Legion of Doom. 8.7/10
